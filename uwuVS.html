uwu-VS is an in-browser video instrument inspired by analog video synthesis techniques.

The animations are written in the OpenGL Shading Language and can be controlled via the provided user interface or external MIDI control. Ten user-defined presets allow for further performance flexibility.

Below you can find a short demonstration in which uwu-VS is modulated by four oscillators sent by Max/MSP. Future versions of uwu-VS will include server-side video export rendering, avoiding the dropped frames caused by video capture software.
